Who I Am

About Me

I am an economist with academic training and practical experience in economic research, policy analysis, and institutional reporting. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the University of Nigeria Nsukka and have worked on policy relevant research projects for government institutions, consulting firms, and development focused initiatives.

My research interests include macroeconomic policy, foreign direct investment, trade and industrial policy, public finance, labor economics, and development economics. I am particularly interested in using empirical methods to analyze real world policy challenges and contribute to evidence-based decision making.

Beyond research, I am actively involved in professional development programs, policy dialogues, and economic capacity building initiatives.
